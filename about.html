About

I’ve always loved technology and design as a hobby, but recently I had what you could call an “aha” moment, when I realised that this thing I did for fun was actually a possible career path.

Once I made that connection I dove deep into the world of web development and am now amazed that I get paid to do what I love every day.

One of my personal goals is to make the web a better place, which is why I have a strong interest in accessibility as well as user experience. I believe every user interaction is an opportunity to connect people and ideas in a meaningful way.

I love discussing tech and the web which is why you’ll often find me at industry events like meetups, workshops and conferences.

If you see me, be sure to come say hi!
